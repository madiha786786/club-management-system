Yoga Day 2025

Theme: "One Earth, One Health"

About the Event

The 11th International Yoga Day was celebrated by the faculty of KMIT on 20th June with great eagerness and enthusiasm. The theme for the International Yoga Day 2025 is "One Earth, One Health". This year's observance emphasized the vital link between personal well-being and the health of the planet.

The event started with a brief introduction about yoga with the resource person Ms. S. Bhavani. She demonstrated stress-relieving breathing exercises and quick workplace posture corrections which were extremely helpful. The entire session was inspiring and motivating.

Yoga has to be imbibed as part of our regular practice to help combat many mental and physical challenges. The event concluded with felicitation of our guest Ms. S. Bhavani and a vote of thanks.

Event Highlights

Expert Guidance

Resource person Ms. S. Bhavani demonstrated yoga techniques

Practical Sessions

Stress-relieving breathing exercises and workplace posture corrections

Theme Focus

Emphasis on the connection between personal well-being and planetary health

Prakalp'25

Annual Project Expo

About the Event

PRAKALP 2025 is the annual flagship project expo of Keshav Memorial Institute of Technology, organized by first-year students. It serves as a platform where ideas from diverse fields converge to shape a better future.

More than just an event, PRAKALP celebrates creativity, innovation, and academic excellence. Students tackle real-world challenges and apply classroom knowledge through hands-on projects.

Expo Features

Interdisciplinary Work

Projects across Mechanical, Electrical, Physics, Chemistry, and English

Technical Demonstration

Showcasing the technical potential of aspiring engineers

Collaborative Spirit

Highlighting teamwork and collaboration among students

Women's Day 2025

About the Event

KMIT proudly celebrates International Women's Day each year on March 8th, honoring the remarkable achievements and contributions of women in our community and beyond. In 2025, our NSS unit organized special events including an empowering walkathon where participants walked together in sarees, symbolizing unity and support for women's rights. The celebration featured inspiring talks and activities focused on gender equality and women's empowerment.

Key Messages:

G Celebrating women's achievements in STEM fields

Promoting gender equality in education and workplace

Recognizing the importance of women's leadership
