Welcome to Clubs Hub

What we do

KMIT Clubs Hub is a dedicated platform that connects students with campus clubs and their activities. We provide an easy way for students to discover and join the clubs they are interested in. All events are created and managed directly by the respective clubs, ensuring authentic and engaging experiences. Faculty members can view and monitor club memberships and access participant details, allowing for smooth coordination and transparency. Our goal is to bring students, clubs, and faculty together in one unified digital space to encourage active participation and vibrant campus life.

How it works

1

Simple Registration

Students, club heads, and faculty register on KMIT Clubs Hub. All details are securely stored in MongoDB for seamless access.

2

Secure Login

Based on the registered credentials, users log in to their respective dashboards — Admin, Student, Club Head, or Faculty — with role-based access.

3

Student Engagement

Students can browse available clubs, join the ones they like, and stay updated on upcoming events created by their clubs.

4

Club Event Creation

Club heads create and manage events for their members, ensuring only relevant and verified activities are hosted.

5

Faculty Oversight

Faculty members can view the list of students in each club, monitor participation, and track event details for smooth supervision.

6

Centralized Management

All operations — from registrations to event updates — are stored and managed in one system, ensuring efficiency and transparency for everyone.
